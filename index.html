Meet the Creator

✨ Hello, I'm Sanjanarao

I've always loved turning a blank page into something beautiful. From decorating notebook covers by hand to creating digital designs for school projects, creativity has been a part of my journey for years.

What started as a hobby in ninth grade eventually grew into a passion for designing templates that help celebrate life's special moments. Today, I create editable Canva templates for weddings, engagements, birthdays, housewarming celebrations, festivals, Christian events, and more. 💖

I enjoy exploring a variety of styles—from elegant and minimalist to traditional, floral, watercolor, cultural, and modern designs. Rather than focusing on just one style, I love creating templates that suit different personalities, traditions, and celebrations. ✨

Every template is designed in Canva with simplicity in mind, making it easy to edit and personalize. If you ever need assistance, I'm always happy to help. 🤍

Thank you for visiting Sanjana Atelier . I'm honored to be a small part of your special moments and celebrations. 🌸
